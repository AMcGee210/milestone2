All You Need to Know Guide as a Beginner Mountain Biker

Inspiration

I got heavily into mountain biking when I was in seventh grade. I began researching bikes and watching endless videos on what bike is best suited for various types of trail riding. I also spent the majority of my free time during the pandemic exploring local trails. Throughout that process I have learned a lot about how to find trails, which bike to buy for each type of trail riding, where to buy the best bike, and how to find others to ride with.

Whats in the Guide

Bikes

A complete guide to all categories of mountain bikes and the strengths and weaknesses of each

Shops

An advisory list of what to think about and look for when choosing what bike shop to buy your new bike from

Trails

Tips and tricks on how and where to find the best trails near you and how to make the most of them

Community

The best ways to find other riders in your area
